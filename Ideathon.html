What is a ideathon?

The ideathon is a dynamic whose purpose is to generate innovative ideas to face or respond to a need. In this dynamic, the aim is to involve a community of participants in solving a development challenge

Description

During the three hours of competition, the teams must work on the development of their ideas, in the facilities of their choice, aiming at the realization of their proposals based on the evaluation criteria established in the terms and conditions.
